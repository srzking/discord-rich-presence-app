Aura

Rich Presence for the web.

Aura shows what you're watching, listening to, or browsing on your Discord profile — automatically. Works across YouTube, Netflix, Spotify, Twitch and more, with no installs or terminals required.

Setup in 2 minutes

  1. Open the Discord Developer Portal and create a new Application.
  2. Go to the Bot tab → Reset Token → copy the token.
  3. Copy the Application ID from General Information (optional, only for images).
  4. Invite the bot to any server you're in (no permissions needed).
  5. Click the Aura icon in your toolbar, open the Account tab and paste both values.
  6. Browse a supported site — your status updates automatically.

To show platform thumbnails, upload images named youtube, netflix, spotify, etc. as Rich Presence assets in your application.

What Aura can do

  • Auto-detect activity on 15+ websites
  • Custom activity override (Playing / Watching / Listening / Competing)
  • Per-site toggles — turn off platforms you don't want shared
  • Auto-idle when you're AFK
  • Skip incognito tabs for privacy
  • One-click on/off switch
  • Online · Idle · DND · Invisible status control
