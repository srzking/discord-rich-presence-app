Aura

Rich Presence for the web.

Aura shows what you're watching, listening to, or browsing on your Discord profile — automatically.

Setup in 1 minute

  1. Open Discord in your browser and log in.
  2. Press F12 → Network tab → click any request → copy the Authorization header.
  3. Click the Aura icon, paste your token in the Account tab.
  4. Hit Login & Connect — your bot/user is detected automatically.
  5. Browse a supported site — your profile activity updates live.
