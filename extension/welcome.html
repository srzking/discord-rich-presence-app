Aura

Rich Presence for the web.

Aura shows what you're watching, listening to, or browsing on your Discord profile — automatically. No account needed, no servers in the middle.

Setup in 1 minute

Open Discord in browserLog in to discord.com on Chrome/Edge/Brave/etc.
Copy your tokenF12 → Network → click any request → copy the Authorization header.
Paste in AuraClick the Aura icon → Account tab → paste token.
Click Login & ConnectAura validates and shows your account name.
Browse normallyOpen YouTube, Spotify, GitHub… your profile updates live.

Tips

  • Your token never leaves your browser. No servers, no DB.
  • Use the master switch in the popup to instantly pause Aura.
  • The Logs tab shows every detection and reconnect event.
  • Apps tab is grouped by category — disable any site you don't want.
