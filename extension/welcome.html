👋 Welcome to WebPresence

Show what you're watching, listening to or browsing on your Discord profile — automatically. Works with YouTube, Netflix, Spotify, Twitch and many more.

Quick setup

  1. Go to the Discord Developer Portal and create a new Application.
  2. Open the Bot tab and click Reset Token to copy your bot token.
  3. Copy your Application ID from the General Information page (optional, only needed if you want images).
  4. Invite the bot to a server you're in (it just needs to be online — no permissions required).
  5. Open the WebPresence popup, paste both values, click Save & Connect.
  6. Open YouTube, Spotify or any supported site — your Discord status will update.

Note: bots show their own presence, so the status appears on the bot account. To show images, upload them as Rich Presence assets in your application using the platform id (e.g. youtube, netflix, spotify).
